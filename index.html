Punch resistance lab condition M0 (kN) Punch resistance slope condition Mmesh (kN)

E) Safety Coefficients

Coefficient applied to resistances (γR) Coefficient applied to destabilization actions (γD) Coefficient applied on the tensile and the punch resistance (γmesh) Coefficient applied on yield stress of bar (γA) Coefficient applied on bond stress (γT)
